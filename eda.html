Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) helps uncover patterns, trends, and relationships in data to support informed decision-making. It enables a deeper understanding of data distribution, detects outliers, and identifies correlations between variables. By visualizing key metrics, EDA reveals hidden insights that can guide business strategies, optimize operations, and predict future trends. It also aids in anomaly detection, ensuring data quality before applying complex models. In real estate, for example, EDA can highlight rent price trends, revenue sources, and unit size variations across states. Ultimately, EDA is a foundational step in data science, helping stakeholders make data-driven decisions efficiently.
